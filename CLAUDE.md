## Conventions (carried from anderson-it)

- Concise voice, no em dashes. Playful/local tone fits this brand.

- Deploy: `git push origin main` → GitHub Pages auto-rebuilds. Repo is **PUBLIC** — no internal notes/emails in tracked files.
